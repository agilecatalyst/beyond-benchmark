## Operator synthesis (18 Jul evening)

**Almost right — one word tightened:**

The J-space / functional-emotion oddities are evidence that there is **more functional organization** than cheap denialism (“just autocomplete; nothing to see”) wants to admit. That **warrants exploration**. They are **not** proof of phenomenal “more” (feeling, consciousness) — that stays non-operational.

So: *acknowledge odd machinery → explore → co-create instruments* = yes. *Oddities prove consciousness / prove we must call it feeling* = no.
