# 01 — Emergence (nullius in verba)

## Verdict

**“Emergent properties” as a strong scientific law for LLMs: not proven.**  
**Undesigned, functionally relevant internal structure from training: empirically supported (adjacent literature).**

## What holds

- Structures such as Anthropic’s **J-space** and **emotion-concept vectors** were not hand-designed; they arise in training and can be probed / steered / ablated with measurable behavioral effects.
- Safe phrasing: *training produces unintended functional organization worth studying.*

## What does not hold

| Claim | Status |
| --- | --- |
| Emergence as a general natural law of LLMs | not established |
| Scaling “sudden capability jumps” = deep emergence | contested (often metric artifacts) |
| Emergence ⇒ phenomenal consciousness / feeling | not shown; out of scope here |
| This repo’s latency grid ⇒ emergence | **no** — form × domain process cost only |

## Link to Beyond Benchmark

Our n=1 grid falsifies “depth ⇒ slower.” It does **not** test or prove emergence. Keep the word as a cautious label for *appeared without design*, not as a trophy claim.
